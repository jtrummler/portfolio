about me


Hey there! I am Jack Rummler - a Florida born, Philadelphia residing lover of all things urban and spatial. I am a sustainability and resilience planning professional turned geospatial data analyst inspired to work at the intersections of urban planning, public policy, and data science. I aspire to work where I can leverage my skillsets in spatial data analysis to build and shift sustainable community outcomes.


Whether watching the social and ecological degradation of my home state to my personal experiences navigating poor built environment infrastructure living with spina bifida, my passion for policy and planning work is driven by a myriad of life experiences. With a Bachelor of Sustainability from the University of Florida and Master of Urban Spatial Analytics from the University of Pennsylvania, I have the dual forces of applied social science research and quantitative spatial analysis to approach policy and planning decision-making with a creative, equitable, and data-oriented lens.


If that piques your interest, my resume, socials, and portfolio are below. Thanks for stopping by!
