about me


Hey there! I am Jack Rummler - a Florida born, Philadelphia residing lover of all things urban and spatial. I currently work as a policy and data analyst at the Defender Association of Philadelphia leveraging data to promote criminal justice reform and social equity, as well as a consultant for the World Bank building geospatial data products for the City Resilience Program.


Whether watching the social and ecological degradation of my home state to my personal experiences navigating poor built environment infrastructure living with spina bifida, my passion for policy and planning work is driven by a myriad of life experiences. With a Bachelor of Sustainability from the University of Florida and Master of Urban Spatial Analytics from the University of Pennsylvania, I have the dual forces of applied social science research with data and spatial analysis to approach policy and planning decision-making with a creative, equitable, and data-forward lens.


If that piques your interest, my resume, socials, and portfolio are below. Thanks for stopping by!
